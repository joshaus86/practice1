Miami Marketta

It could be the back streets of Thailand, Barcelona or New York City but it is right here in Miami on the Gold Coast. Marketta Street Food, a laneway dining experience. 25 food vendors of all nationalities in the one space, take your entree from spain, your main from Thailand and sweets from france. Seating for over 650 people at any one time AND always awesome live music. ​ Open to the public for Street Food every Wednesday, Friday and Saturday. Check out facebook events page for all of our live music, special food and our new Gin Parlour events
